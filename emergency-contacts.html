Emergency Contacts

Here, you can provide emergency contact information for situations when a person meets with an accident.

  • Emergency Services: Dial 911
  • Police Emergency: Dial 100
  • Ambulance: Dial 102 or 108
  • Highway Helpline: Dial 1033
  • Fire Emergency: Dial 101
  • Women's Helpline: Dial 181
  • Child Helpline: Dial 1098
  • Tourist Helpline: Dial 1363
